Domaine Vincent Paris Saint-Joseph

Rhone, France
$23 | Alc. % 12.5
The nose is perfumed and perhaps a touch wild with cherry and raspberry aromas match by crushed dry herbs, spice and pepper. The palate is medium bodied and subtle, requiring more than a quick glance. Red berried fruits, notes of kirsch and whole-bunch derived forest floor – all beautifully detailed and precise – before a bright acid finish that is classically northern Rhône.

Description

Vincent Paris is a rising young star of Cornas, a region itself on the rise. Vincent’s first vintage was in 1997, made with his uncle Robert Michel, one of Cornas’ finest growers. Vincent owns 6 hectares of vineyards, up 90 years old, primarily in Cornas with a small holding in Saint Joseph.
