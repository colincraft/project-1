The grapes for this medium-to-full bodied red were sourced from two regions of France - the Languedoc in the south of France and the Gers in Gascony, southeast of Bordeaux. A real all-rounder red, it bursts with juicy cassis and blackberry fruit, mixed with spicy vanilla oak mingled with hints of moch, all followed by a long, smooth finish.
